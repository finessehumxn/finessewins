Questions

Straight answers before you start.

Do I need past performance to bid?
No. Many solicitations weigh past performance, but plenty of set-aside and simplified-acquisition opportunities are winnable without a federal track record. FinesseWins's zero past-performance mode reframes your private-sector, subcontract, and nonprofit work into relevant, credible narratives — and flags the opportunities where a first-time bidder has the best shot.
Which certifications do I need?
None are required to bid on full-and-open opportunities. But certifications like WOSB, 8(a), HUBZone, MBE, and DBE unlock set-aside contracts and strengthen your positioning. FinesseWins works whether or not you're certified, and helps you weave any certifications you do hold into your win themes so they count toward your evaluation.
Is my data secure?
Your company profile and proposal content are encrypted in transit and at rest, and are never sold or shared. Your documents belong to you — you can export or delete them at any time. FinesseWins only uses your inputs to generate your proposals, not to train shared models.
Can I edit what the AI writes?
Always. FinesseWins produces a strong first draft — you stay in full control. Edit any section inline, regenerate paragraphs, adjust tone and length, and review everything before you export. The final DOCX is yours to refine before submission.
What agencies can I bid to?
Any agency that posts to SAM.gov — federal civilian and defense — plus Arizona state and local opportunities through the AZ APP Portal (on Pro and above). FinesseWins adapts each proposal to the specific solicitation's structure, evaluation factors, and formatting requirements, whichever agency issued it.
