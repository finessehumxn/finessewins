Section 1 · Scope of platform

Everything between "where do I even find bids?" and "we won."

Federal procurement is in the middle of its biggest rewrite in 40 years — old templates, stale clause numbers, and yesterday's playbooks are how first-timers get ruled non-responsive. You need tooling built for how contracting works now.

Find

Every bid site, one search

SAM.gov, Grants.gov, FedConnect, DLA DIBBS, GSA eBuy, and state & local portals searched concurrently and de-duplicated. Search by keyword, NAICS, or set-aside — with per-source status so you know what's live.

Score

Bid IQ — know your odds

A transparent 0–100 winnability score built from real federal award history, plus a price-to-win band showing what winning bids actually cost, incumbent analysis, and market intel per NAICS and agency.

Watch

Bid Radar

Save your trades in plain English — "cleaning," "trucking," "catering" — and the platform sweeps every bid site twice a day, drops matches into one feed, and emails you digests. Nothing closes without you knowing.

Draft

AI proposals — even with zero past performance

Full Technical, Past Performance, and Price volumes, compliance-checked against the solicitation, exported as a submission-ready Word doc. Zero-past-performance mode is built for your very first bid.

Decode

Toolkit

Paste a dense RFP and get it in plain English. Generate a capability statement and export it to Word. Check which set-asides you're actually eligible for before you waste a cycle.

Track

Amendments, deadlines, pipeline

Track any solicitation and get email alerts when it changes — amendments, Q&A, deadline shifts. Your whole pipeline, live stats, and upcoming deadlines on one dashboard.
